About Me

THE MIND
BEHIND THE Content

"I started as an editor — learning exactly what good content looks like from the inside. Now I use that foundation to manage and build pipelines that consistently deliver."

I'm Karmakar Media — a storyteller and YouTube Manager & Strategist who loves bringing ideas to life. My background in editing gave me an instinct for pacing, structure, and what viewers actually respond to. That same instinct now drives how I manage entire production workflows.

From scripting and editor briefing to managing 10+ editors on 3-hour videos, I keep the pipeline moving. I run weekly team syncs, track deadlines and availability, review edits, give structured feedback, and handle every upload — titles, thumbnails, and posting.
